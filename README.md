# Zomato-Delay-Reduction-Analysis-
Zomato Delay Reduction Analysis As a Business Analyst
Problem: Customers are complaining that orders arrive late.
Solution: Finding reason of the delay of the orders and how to improve it
Operations Manager wants:
To know which restaurants cause delays.
Customer Support Manager reports:
Many complaints about late deliveries.
CEO wants:
Delivery time reduced by 20%.
# Business Requirement
First: Track average delivery time.
Second: Identify restaurants causing delays.
Third: Track cancellation rates.
Fourth: Monitor delivery partner performance.


# Basic chain of process
Customer Orders Food
↓
Restaurant Accepts Order
↓
Food Prepared
↓
Delivery Partner Assigned
↓
Food Delivered

- Average Delivery Time: Result: 40 Mins
Target: 30mins
- Restaurant Analysis: Insight:
Domino's causes most delays.

As an Operations Manager, i have to find out restaurants with high delivery delays.
# Finding
1. Domino's has the highest delivery time.
2. Orders above 50 minutes receive poor ratings.
3. 0% of cancellations occur after 40 minutes.
# Solution
1. Assign more delivery partners during peak hours
2. Monitor restaurant preparation time.
3. Send alerts for orders exceeding 35 minutes.
